TESLA COIL

WRITE UP

Not a lot of people know what exactly is a tesla coil and how it works. Basically, it’s a device that is exciting and educative at the same time. Keeping that in mind, I proposed this very project.
Well, as everything to be sold requires target customers, we also have a target group in mind.. The better part here is that our target audience is pretty general. As mentioned earlier, this project is an educational and entertainment device in one. So, our target group includes 5yr olds who will get excited by this device. It also includes high school and college students who are interested in learning and innovating existing techniques and technology. It also includes individuals who are in their midlife and are excited about new innovations. So with this huge target group in mind, we want to launch our product in the market.
Regarding our current progress, we have been able to make a basic slayer exciter circuit and successfully tried and tested it In labs. We are currently working on the manufacture of a single coil musical tesla coil. The designs for the same have been made. The last stage includes a larger setup with a much larger power output and more coils to create proper music with vocals and bass.
